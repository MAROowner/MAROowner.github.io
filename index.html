Privacy Policy

1. Introduction
This privacy policy describes how we collect, use and protect the information you provide when you use our Telegram mini app for crypto game. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information.

2. Collection and use of data
We collect user data through the Telegram API to provide functionality only when necessary and after receiving your explicit consent. We inform users about the purposes of data collection and processing methods. All actions with data are possible only after receiving your consent.

3. Purposes of data collection
We collect and process the following user data:

Nick in Telegram
Number of earned points
Time spent in game
Information about purchases in the domestic store
Game progress
Who have you invited to the game
Leader board
The number of your referrals

This data is used to:

Synchronization of progress between different devices
Improving the gaming experience and ensuring the correct operation of the game
Providing the opportunity to participate in future competitions for cryptocurrency

4. Data protection
We take all reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure or destruction. We use modern data protection methods and regularly review our security practices.

5. User consent
By using our Telegram mini app, you agree to the terms of this privacy policy. We collect and process your data only after receiving your explicit consent.

6. Changes to the privacy policy
We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. We will notify you of any changes by posting a new version of the policy on this page.

7. Contacts
If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us via our manager @FLAP_Support.
